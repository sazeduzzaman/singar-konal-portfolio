Somnur Monir Konal is an award-winning singer-songwriter from Bangladesh. She began her musical journey at a young age, trained in Hindustani Classical Music, and rose to fame after winning the Channel i Shera Kontho competition in 2009. She has since released numerous hit songs and received multiple national and international awards.

Konal's music spans multiple genres including modern Bengali pop, folk fusion, classical, and film soundtracks. She is known for her versatility and ability to blend traditional Bengali melodies with contemporary arrangements.
